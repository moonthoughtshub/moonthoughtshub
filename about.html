Our Story

About MoonThoughts Hub

A daily sanctuary for the endlessly curious.

Where it started

MoonThoughts Hub began as a simple idea: the world is full of extraordinary stories, forgotten wisdom, and mind-bending facts — and most of them never make it into everyday conversation.

We built a system to change that. Every day, across 9 carefully chosen content streams, MoonThoughts Hub delivers a piece of the world you may never have encountered — history that rewrites what you thought you knew, philosophy that still holds centuries later, science that reframes reality, and mysteries that no one has solved yet.

What we post

Each day we publish across Instagram, Pinterest, and LinkedIn. Every post is a carousel — three slides, three ideas, one theme. Here are the 9 streams we draw from:

  • History — On this day across centuries. The events and people that shaped the world.
  • Motivation — Words that move. Inspiration rooted in truth, not platitude.
  • Mind-Expanding — Space, physics, neuroscience. Facts that shift your perspective.
  • Philosophy — Stoic, Buddhist, Existentialist. Ancient minds, modern relevance.
  • Psychology — The hidden forces shaping every decision you make.
  • Mythology — Greek, Norse, Egyptian. The stories civilizations told themselves.
  • Famous Firsts — The first time in history. Milestones that changed everything.
  • Unsolved Mysteries — Cases and phenomena that still have no explanation.
  • Nature & Biology — The natural world is stranger than any fiction.

Our commitment to accuracy

Every piece of content we publish is reviewed for factual accuracy before it goes live. We don't fabricate quotes, misattribute ideas, or present speculation as fact. Where something is uncertain, we say so. Where mythology and science diverge, we make the distinction clear.

We believe curiosity deserves honesty.

The name

The moon has always been humanity's companion in the dark — a constant, quiet presence overhead while we asked our biggest questions. MoonThoughts Hub is that same space: a place to sit with ideas, let them expand, and come away seeing a little more clearly.
