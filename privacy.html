Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

MoonThoughts Hub ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at moonthoughtshub.com and associated social media accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website and automated content publishing system.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This site does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal information from visitors beyond what is described below.

Information we collect

Website visitors: This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information from visitors.

Social media: When you interact with our content on Instagram (likes, comments, follows), that interaction is governed by Meta's privacy policy. We do not receive your personal data from those interactions beyond the aggregate metrics the platform makes available to content creators.

Third-party services: Our automated posting system uses the following third-party services:

  • Meta (Instagram Graph API) — used to publish content to our Instagram Business account. See Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudinary — used for temporary image hosting required by the Instagram API. Images are not stored beyond what is necessary for posting. See Cloudinary's Privacy Policy.
  • Pollinations.ai — used to generate background images for posts. Only image description prompts are sent; no personal data is involved. See Pollinations.ai.
  • Wikipedia API — used to fetch publicly available factual content for historical and educational posts.

How we use information

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. The third-party services listed above are used solely to operate our automated content publishing pipeline.

Content generation

All content posted by MoonThoughts Hub is generated and reviewed locally on our own hardware using a local AI language model (Ollama). Post content is not sent to external AI services or cloud-based language model providers. The only external data transmissions are image prompts to Pollinations.ai and final published content to the social media platforms listed above.

Data retention

This website does not store visitor data. Published social media content is retained on the respective platforms subject to their own retention policies.

Your rights

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have rights regarding personal data under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. As we do not collect personal data from website visitors, these rights have limited practical application here. For interactions with our social media accounts, please refer to the relevant platform's privacy settings.

Children's privacy

Our content is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

External links

This website contains links to external sites (Instagram and others). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of this website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
